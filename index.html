How It Works

From enquiry to accepted
proposal in minutes

SafariFlow handles the heavy lifting so your clients get faster responses and you get more bookings.

1
📧

Enquiry Arrives

A client enquires — via your website form, SafariBookings, or direct email. SafariFlow captures and structures the request automatically.

⚡ Instant capture
2
🧠

Engine Builds

Our intelligent engine selects destinations, sequences the route, slots your preferred accommodation and prices everything using your exact inventory and markup rules.

⚡ Under 60 seconds
3
✨

AI Narrates

The itinerary is handed to AI which writes a compelling, personalised travel story for your client — not a generic template, a real narrative built around their trip.

⚡ Personalised copy
4
👁️

You Review

You review the proposal in your portal — view the itinerary, check the pricing, preview the client-facing page. Approve in one click.

⚡ 2 minute review
5
📱

Client Receives

Your client receives a stunning mobile-first proposal page — not a PDF attachment. They can accept, request changes, or ask questions directly.

⚡ Instant delivery
6
💰

Invoice & Receipt

Client accepts — invoice is generated and sent automatically. Payment confirmed — receipt issued instantly. Zero manual work from you.

⚡ Fully automated
Features

Everything your safari
business needs

One platform. Every tool. Built specifically for East African safari agents.

🧭

Intelligent Route Engine

Our engine knows your destinations, your preferred routes, your accommodation rankings and your business rules. It builds logical, circular itineraries that reflect how you actually think — not generic AI guesswork. You define the rules once, the engine follows them forever.

CORE ENGINE
✨

AI Narration

Every proposal is written personally for your client — their name, their trip, their experience level. Compelling travel writing that converts.

AI POWERED
📱

Web Proposals

Shareable proposal links — not PDF attachments. Beautiful, mobile-first pages with hero images, day cards, pricing and built-in accept/changes buttons.

📄

Auto Invoicing & Receipts

Client accepts — invoice sent automatically. Payment confirmed — receipt issued instantly. Deposit and balance tracking built in.

📊

Cost Breakdown

Every quote includes an internal cost breakdown — your buying price, markup and profit per line item. Your clients never see it, you always do.

🏨

Priority Inventory

Rank your preferred properties 1, 2, 3 per destination. The engine always picks your first choice — falling back only when needed.

⚙️

Business Rules

Define your constraints — families never in tented camps, balloon rides need 2+ Mara nights, beach always last. The engine never breaks your rules.

🔗

SafariBookings Ready

Seamlessly manage enquiries from SafariBookings alongside your direct bookings — all in one place, same workflow.

PREMIUM
👥

Multi-User

Add your team. Multiple agent logins under one agency account. Everyone working from the same inventory and rules.
